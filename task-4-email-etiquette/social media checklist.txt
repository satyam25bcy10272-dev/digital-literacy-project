Social Media Do’s & Don’ts

✅ Do’s:
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments
Share useful and positive content (projects, achievements, learning)
Use privacy settings to control who can see your information
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news
Build a professional network (connect with peers, seniors, mentors)

❌ Don’ts:
Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Avoid sharing personal details like phone number, address, OTP, etc.
Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
Avoid posting anything that can harm your reputation or career
Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages (can be scams)
